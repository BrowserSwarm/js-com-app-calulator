My collegiate Master's Thesis was titled The Effects of Microblogging in the Classroom on Communication (not the best title huh? haha oh well).

This research aimed to determine if different mediums of communication (Twitter was used) could increase communication between students and their teachers and also amongst their fellow students.

For this research I created a shitty (now that I look back on it), quickly put together PHP app to help me calculate this for all the students participating.

Since then...

...I have been working on creating a simplified test, built completely in JavaScript.

What is Communication Apprehension?

Communication apprehension (CA) is defined as an “individual’s level of fear or anxiety associated with either real or anticipated communication with another person or persons” - McCroskey
